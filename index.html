Gleam Project

We believe
some people and things
are worth being quietly protected.

Every year,
we discreetly send a gift from Massform
to those who love to explore and walk with curiosity in solitude.

It will never appear in any promotion,
only accompanied by a small note:
「You are not alone.」

Perhaps it will reach a student
who stays after school, alone, tinkering with circuits.

Or an engineer,
who—years after leaving the lab—continues to experiment in a garage.

Or a creator,
tucked away in a rented room,
exploring the universe with worn-out instruments.

Perhaps, it will find you.

*Expiration date: As long as we are still alive.
