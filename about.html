*The Physicist's Pet Crow

We come from two worlds.

- Our Story -

He is a rational physicist,
spending his days with numbers, curves,
and the company of a pet crow.

I am a perceptual designer,
wanting those overlooked physical wonders
to be seen and touched.

Before we met,
his inventions lay quietly in the corners of the laboratory.
And I always felt that if they could not be expressed,
it was as if they had never existed at all.

We both know:
In this world, there are always some
who can sense the hidden beauty of reality—
from the curves of an equation,
from the play of light and shadow.
They may be rare,
often misunderstood,
but they have always been there.
And so have we,
hoping to leave behind something
that proves their existence.

That is why we began to collaborate:
Not to produce more objects,
but to let invisible forces and phenomena
stay by people's side—
in ways that astonish,
and are worth being understood again and again.

This is the beginning of Massform:
A place where rationality and perception intertwine.
